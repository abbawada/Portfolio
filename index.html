About

I am a senior at USC studying Economics with a minor in Mathematical Finance, specializing in Connected Devices & Making through the Viterbi School of Engineering. In a world where I wasn’t obsessed with tech, I’d probably be writing novels.

I love storytelling in all its forms — from fiction and films to games like Red Dead Redemption 2 — and I’m currently reading The Story of the Lost Child by Elena Ferrante. Outside the lab, I’m running, biking through the city, making a short film for Nike with friends, or watching Arsenal and Lewis Hamilton. My work is grounded in faith, curiosity, and a desire to build things that genuinely help people, whether that’s hardware, software, or community through volunteering.
